Hack@Brown

2019

  1. Hack@Brown 2019

    Hack@Brown is an annual hackathon run by students at Brown University and RISD. This year, I worked with 6 other talented designers to create the visual branding for the Hack@Brown student organization.

  2. Style Guide

    Hack@Brown aims for Hackathon to be more approachable and inviting. With this year’s theme being Recess, we decided to work the visuals around a playground. This style guide was created by the design team collaboratively.

  3. Web UI Design

    By incorporating 3D visuals, the Hack@Brown 2019 web is playful and inviting. This year’s hackathon encourages everyone, no matter how experienced, to participate in trying a new form of technology to create something inventive and beautiful while having fun.

  4. Phone UI Design

    By incorporating 3D visuals, the Hack@Brown 2019 web is playful and inviting. This year’s hackathon encourages everyone, no matter how experienced, to participate in trying a new form of technology to create something inventive and beautiful while having fun.

  5. 3D Illustration Design

    4 designers, including myself, were responsible for designing the illustrations on the landing page. I was responsible for the sponsor section of the page.

  6. Nametags

    I was assigned to design the look of the nametags that would be given on the day of the Hackathon.

  7. Workshop Banners

    I was also responsible for these 3 workshop banners’ illustrations. Each illustration is inspired by the company’s mission.
