Amoragram

2018

  1. Amoragram App

    Amoragram is an app designed to communicate with lost lovers from the Outside. The interface is a digital platform for the living to maintain a connection with their deceased partner. This app is a critique on how people grieve in a technology oriented society and how people would turn to any means of reconnection during desperate times. The chatbot, named Amor, is what users will be communicating with.

  2. Phone App Userflow

    This tutorial allows the user to be familiar with the limited 4 gestures and its meaning. The question asked by the user will be recorded and the responses will be shown as single word answers. The users will send recordings of their questions and Amor will respond with gestures. It's technically chance-oriented and responses are arbitrary but it conveys how users will still feel a legitimate connection with this unpersonalized default bot.

  3. Advertisement Flyers

    There are four different flyers displaying the four different gestures that are used in the app.
