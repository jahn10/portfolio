Providence

2018

  1. Providence (Web)

    This is an event searching website aimed to rebrand the city ‘Providence’ in Rhode Island by displaying the it’s vision. This city honors the voices of the people and collectively thinks about the city’s future direction. Providence is a place built upon innovation and creativity and for many centuries, many Providence residents take pride in defining the city by its creative entrepreneurship. By using a gradient of blue and yellow, the Providence flag’s colors, it symbolizes the ever changing state of Providence.
