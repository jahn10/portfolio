Hack@Brown

2019

Hack@Brown is an annual hackathon run by students at Brown University and RISD. This year, I worked with 6 other talented designers to create the visual branding for the Hack@Brown student organization.

Hack@Brown aims for Hackathon to be more approach-able and inviting. With this year’s theme being Recess, we decided to work the visuals around a playground.

Branding

UI/UX

3D Visuals

By incorporating 3D visuals, the Hack@Brown 2019 web is playful and inviting, encouraging everyone to partic-ipate in trying a new form of technology to create something inventive and beautiful while having fun.

4 designers, including myself, were responsible for designing the illustrations on the landing page. I was responsible for the sponsor section of the page.

Collateral

I was assigned to design the look of the nametags that would be given on the day of the Hackathon.I was also responsible for 3 workshop banners’ illustrations. Each illustration is inspired by the company’s mission.
