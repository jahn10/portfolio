Primary Secondary

2018

  1. Set of 6 Books

    This research studies the representation of color in literature. The name of my project is called ‘Primary Secondary’ because I chose all primary and secondary colors to research on, in terms of how they are portrayed in literature.

    Furthermore, the primary is in reference to the term ‘primary source’ which is the literature in this case. The secondary would be the secondary sources, which would unravel in my books.

    It is printed using risograph as it uses spot coloring instead of the conventional CMYK inks to produce print. It has its own range of colors unique to its process, colors that conventio-nal printers are unable to replicate. The richness of the ink and it’s texture emphasizes the impact of my work conceptually as the viewer is overwhelmed with the colored ink on the same colored paper.
